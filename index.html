    About Us

      The Versatile Information Science Technical Aspirants ( VISTA ), a club in NMAMIT Nitte, has been formed by the students of Information Science and Engineering for the benefit of students with an intention to provide a platform to participate in co-curricular activities and to give opportunity to mingle with other students. The motive of this association is to bring out the hidden talent and to involve each member in various activities so that the youth exuberance should not be confined to class room only.

      Since its inception, the association is fully engaged in many events. The main such events are as below:
    • Organizing workshops on topics beyond syllabus or curriculum
    • Celebration of Teachers day, Fresher’s day, festivals
    • Cultural events and competitions
    • Interaction and spreading knowledge of creating websites and computers among school students
    • Technical talks and Alumni interaction
    • Encouraging students to participate in co-curricular activities and sports

    Our Mission

    To make NMAMIT a pivotal node in the diverse sets of industrial activities

    Our Vision

    Excellence in information science and engineering through a strong research and teaching environment that addresses the emerging global challenges and market demands

    Purpose

    VISTA is official Student chapter of the Department of Information Science& Engineering, NMAMIT ,Nitte. It caters the overall needs of its student by helping them Technically as well as Non Technically by conducting supporting programs through the help of Industry / Alumni so that students get the right guidance and assistance to be up to date with current trends in the market and thereby Industry ready.

    Talk on importance of passion

    Department of information Science & Engineering student association VISTA conducted an Alumni talk on 13th July 2020 by Ms. Nikhita Prabhu who is one of the Alumni of Nitte. The talk started by her explaining about the word passion and how it affects one’s life. She told that it is very important to build a passion because it is what will give one happiness in life when one gets stressed out and having passion in life will keep one motivated, goal oriented and happy. She mentioned how she expanded her passion by sharing it in different online platforms. The talk stressed on the point to identify our passion and talents and create ways to utilise them. Overall, it’s very important to adopt our passion and live happily.

    Talk on Career guidance

    Department of information Science & Engineering student association VISTA conducted an Alumni talk on 16th May 2020 by Ms. Divya Kamath, who is one of the Alumni of Nitte and currently working with Xamarine Inc. Bangalore. The talk began by sharing her work experiences and the challenges faced in the IT industry. A clear idea about the various product and service-based companies. She focused on the importance of having a good network which would help the students to connect to the outer world. She spoke about the trending areas and technical languages which are currently being used in most of the companies and also tips for improving one’s profile. She stressed on the fact that how important it is to build a strong and crisp resume. There was a Q&A session where the students as well as the lecturers cleared their doubts related to the topic. Towards the end she gave a demo on how to make a project using Xamarin language and told us about the environment setup. Overall, it was an interactive and informative talk which would help the students become successful engineers.

    Journey of Passion to Success.

    Department of information Science & Engineering student association VISTA conducted an Alumni talk on 19th May 2020 by Ms. Prerana Nayak, who is one of the Alumni of Nitte and currently working with SAP Labs, Bangalore. The talk began by her where she shared her passion and how she is still exposed to all those art forms. She spoke about the current situation and how important it is to utilise this time to explore oneself not only in the technical field but also in the non-technical field. The talk focused on how necessary it is to discover our passion which would lead us to a relaxed state of mind as one will be happy while pursuing it. She then told us to note down the daily thoughts which would help us have an awareness about things happening around us. In the end it was suggested to make the most of this pandemic by upgrading our skill sets. Overall, it was an inspiring talk which would help students balance their work life and passion together.

    Talk on career options

    Department of information Science & Engineering student association VISTA conducted an Alumni talk on 10th August 2020 by Mr. Sushrut Tendulkar is our Alumni and currently working as a Senior consultant in a Web analytics company in Bangalore. He began the talk by speaking about various career options. Firstly, he spoke about a web analyst and mentioned about various skill sets required. He told us about the different platforms where we can learn these skills for free. The second option he mentioned was about Conversion Rate Optimization Specialist where he gave a clear idea about it by giving various examples and he informed us about the tools required to achieve it. Finally, he told about Implementation Engineers and gave insights about the companies that would hire these students. Overall, it was a very helpful and educative talk.
